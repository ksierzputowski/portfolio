Welcome!

My name is Kamil. I am 24 years old and I have a Master's degree in law. For several years I have been looking for a profession that would give me satisfaction and prospects for further development. I was a salesman, I had my own business and I also tried my hand at law firms, but in all these professions I did not feel that I was in the right place.

At the beggining of year 2021, a friend of mine (Magento developer) offered to help me learn php and since then my programming journey began. At first I started with Codeacademy php course. My next step was to write my own application. This was definetly the hardes and the longest part. I needed a lot of time to understand the mvc architecture, but it gave me a lot of useful skills.


Currently I have started learning Magento 2 and this is the direction I want to develop in. At the beginning of October, I left my casual job and took up programming with full commitment. I definitely feel that this is the right time to start an internship or full-time job as a junior php programmer. I am sure it could bring a lot of mutual benefits for me and my future employer.
